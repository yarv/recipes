Ingredients:
 - two tablespoons of olive oil
 - one medium yellow onion, or two to three shallots, chopped
 - 6 to 8 anchovy fillets, with oil
 - one beefsteak tomato, peeled and diced
 - one can (28 oz) of diced tomatoes, partially drained
 - four cloves of garlic, finely chopped
 - two teaspoons of capers
 - two pinches of oregano
 - cayenne, to taste
 - salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
1. First, heat a medium/large saucepan with olive oil, on medium. 
   Throw in onions, stirring occasionally, until yellow/brown. 

2. Throw in garlic and anchovies, bring heat to medium/high and 
   stir well. 

3. Throw in tomatoes (both canned and fresh), bring down to a 
   simmer and stir well.

4. Add capers and seasonings. Let simmer for 15 minutes. 

5. Serve with pasta or rice.
